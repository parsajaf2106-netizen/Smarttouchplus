About Smart Touch +

We're a Richmond Hill-based IT consulting firm dedicated to building secure, resilient technology foundations for businesses across the GTA.

Our Mission

Technology should be an asset, not an obstacle.

Founded in Richmond Hill, Ontario, Smart Touch + has spent the past 8 years built on a simple premise: business technology should move your company forward, not slow it down.

Too many businesses struggle with reactive IT support — waiting for things to break, suffering downtime, and facing unpredictable costs. We take a proactive, enterprise-grade approach tailored for small to mid-sized organizations.

Today, we serve companies across the Greater Toronto Area, delivering help desk support, network management, and cybersecurity that let our clients focus on what they do best: running their business.
